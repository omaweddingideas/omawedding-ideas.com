about

We are a 5.25” x 8.25” magazine directory called OMA Wedding Ideas. It’s monthly printed, with a beautiful glossy paper, high quality ink and full color ads. Our mag is free to our targeted readers, and it has a small cost but great value to our advertisers. We distribute 7,500 to 10,000 mags all over the Orlando Metropolitan Area, at industry businesses, high traffic places, bridal shows, and at our brides doorsteps! Our Online circulation, however, is unlimited in quantity and reach. Our readers pick our mags with a purpose! They need to plan their wedding or someone else’s wedding locally, and we bring the wedding pros in the area to them. There are no distractions here, our main focus is on the advertisers and the amazing products and services they offer! We are a true resourceful guide to YOU.
